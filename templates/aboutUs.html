PROJECT OVERVIEW

The IPL Auction System is a web application that manages the data of the auction process of the Indian Premier League (IPL), a popular professional Twenty20 cricket league in India.

The application aims to provide a user-friendly platform for IPL Admin to stimulate the IPL auction process, allowing the IPL teams to bid for and acquire players for their respective teams. With its intuitive user interface and efficient data management system, the IPL Auction System simplifies the auction process, enabling team owners to make informed decisions and build competitive teams.


TECHNOLOGIES USED:

Python, Flask, SQLAlchemy
